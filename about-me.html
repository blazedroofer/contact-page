About Josh

From laborer to business owner – built on hard work, integrity, and a commitment to excellence.

My Journey

My journey with Roof It began from the ground up—literally. I started in the roofing industry as a laborer, hauling materials and learning the ropes one shingle at a time. From there, I worked my way up to become a skilled shingler, honing my craft on rooftops across countless projects. Now, as I launch my own venture, that hands-on progression gives me a real edge: I know the job inside and out, from the physical demands to the fine details that make a roof last.

I've been in the trenches, so I prioritize quality workmanship, efficiency, and treating my team right—advantages that desk-bound owners often overlook. Unlike big companies that sub out work to the lowest bidder, potentially sacrificing quality and accountability, I handle everything in-house. Going with someone like me means you're getting a roofer who's built it all from scratch—the processes, the website, even a custom pricing estimator spreadsheet I coded in Python to ensure transparent, accurate quotes.

What Drives Me

Before roofing, I played university football, where I learned the value of discipline, teamwork, and pushing through adversity to achieve excellence. I carry those principles into every job, striving to be the best roofer I can be by focusing on preparation, execution, and continuous improvement. I'm constantly learning new skills to sharpen my craft, whether it's the latest roofing techniques or tech tools to streamline operations.

It's what sets Roof It apart—I'm not just a roofer; I'm also a dad to my 11-year-old son, which keeps me grounded and reminds me to approach every project with care and integrity.

The Roof-It Difference

I've watched too many company owners who never swing a hammer mismanage funds—skipping essential tools and better equipment for their workers while splurging on lavish vacations and oversized trucks they don't need. That's not me. At Roof It, every dollar goes toward building a stronger team and delivering top-notch results for our clients.

I treat every roof as if it were my own home, and you'll always have me personally on the job—no cutouts or handoffs to strangers. Let's get your roof done right.
